Who we are

Who We Are

About BE Epitaxy Semiconductor Co., Ltd. was established in December 2020 and focuses on the design and research and development of silicon photonics platforms. We have gathered a team full of creativity and professionalism to constantly pursue excellence in the field of optics. By integrating the latest technology and innovative thinking, we are committed to developing market-leading products and solutions to bring innovative and excellent experiences to our customers.

What We Believe

Corporate Culture

Innovation, employee care, excellent quality, and customer commitment are the business philosophy and values of BE-Epitaxy Semiconductor Co., Ltd. We firmly believe that innovation is the driving force. We uphold the values of development, cooperation, and continuous learning, constantly challenging tradition and pursuing excellence. Company History

  • BE Epitaxy Semiconductor Co., Ltd. officially established.
